Here are some of our Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our ice cream machines worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

2. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the machine is in its original condition. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

3. Do the machines come with a warranty?

All our machines come with a 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are also available for purchase.

4. Can I get training for using the machines?

Absolutely! The first training is free for all customers, also we offer additional training sessions. Check our Training Schedule for upcoming sessions.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For large orders, please contact our sales team for custom payment options.
