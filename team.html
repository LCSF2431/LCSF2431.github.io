Our Story

Penguin Ice Cream Maker started over 15 years ago with a single mission: to revolutionize the way ice cream is made and enjoyed. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a global brand trusted by entrepreneurs and businesses worldwide.

Our journey has been guided by one simple principle: provide the best tools and support for your success. Today, we are proud to support small businesses and ice cream enthusiasts across the globe, helping them turn their dreams into reality.

Did You Know?

Penguin Ice Cream Maker has won numerous awards for innovation and sustainability in the food industry. Our machines are designed to reduce energy consumption while maintaining top-notch performance.

We also support local communities by donating a portion of our profits to programs that teach young entrepreneurs the art of ice cream making.

Penguin Ice Cream Maker has proudly sold over 10,000 machines worldwide, helping more than 7,000 small businesses and entrepreneurs turn their passion for ice cream into thriving ventures. Our commitment to quality and innovation continues to empower businesses of all sizes to succeed in the frozen dessert industry.
